About Me

Hi I am Qayz Nurani, I am 15 years old and I attend Palm Desert High School. Ever since I was 8 I have been interested in computers and the software and hardware involved in running them. I have built and taken apart a few computers and I am always learning about the newest parts coming out. I have also built a few games in both Unreal Engine and Unity. I love building computers and making software.
